# Privacy Policy for Guess Party
  
  **Last Updated:** May 17, 2026
  
  ## Introduction
  
  Guess Party ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy
  Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our
  mobile application.
  
  ## Information We Collect
  
  ### Information You Provide
  - **Username**: When you create or join a game
  - **Game Data**: Your game progress, scores, and gameplay statistics
  
  ### Automatically Collected Information
  - **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
  - **Usage Data**: App features used, game sessions
  - **Technical Data**: Crash reports and performance data
  
  ## How We Use Your Information
  
  We use the collected information to:
  - Provide and maintain the game service
  - Enable multiplayer functionality
  - Track game progress and scores
  - Improve app performance and user experience
  - Fix bugs and technical issues
  
  ## Data Storage
  
  Your data is stored securely using Supabase (our backend service provider). We implement
  appropriate security measures to protect your information.
  
  ## Third-Party Services
  
  We use the following third-party services:
  - **Supabase**: Backend database and authentication
  - **Sentry** (if applicable): Error tracking and monitoring
  
  These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.
  
  ## Data Retention
  
  - Game data is retained while your account is active
  - You can request deletion of your data at any time
  
  ## Your Rights
  
  You have the right to:
  - Access your personal data
  - Request correction of your data
  - Request deletion of your data
  - Withdraw consent for data processing
  
  ## Children's Privacy
  
  Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from
  children under 13 without parental consent.
  
  ## Changes to This Policy
