Guess Party is a social-deduction game for groups of friends.

Choose Online Mode for synchronized multiplayer on separate devices, or
Shared-Device Mode for pass-and-play role reveals and voting on one connected
device. Every mode requires an internet connection and an authenticated session.

Create a room, invite players, discover clues, submit hints, vote for the
imposter, and compare scores across rounds. English and Arabic are supported.
