Guess Party is a connected social-deduction party game for friends.

Play synchronized Online Mode on separate devices or pass one connected device
around in Shared-Device Mode. Both modes require internet access and an
authenticated session. Create rooms, exchange clues, vote, and score multiple
rounds. English and Arabic are supported.
